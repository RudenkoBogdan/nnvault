The [[Approximation Error]] measures the fundamental ability of the network architecture to capture the target data relationship.

It gauges how close representable functions can get to the optimal approximator.

Determined solely by hyperparameters: depth, width, activation choice.

Per [[Universal Approximation Theorem]], it approaches zero with unlimited depth/width.
