The main goal of [[Regularization in Neural Networks|regularization]] is reducing [[Generalization Error]]—the gap between train and test error.

It:
- Reduces [[Weight Divergence]] with scarce data
- Prevents extreme gradients/NaNs
- Smooths loss landscape for global minima

Divided into explicit and implicit.
