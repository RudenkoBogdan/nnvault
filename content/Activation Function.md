An [[Activation Function|activation function]] $\sigma: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}$ introduces nonlinearity into the neural network model.

Per the [[Universal Approximation Theorem]], a nonlinear activation with sufficient width/depth approximates any continuous function on a compact set arbitrarily well.

Common activations:
- [[Sigmoid]]
- [[ReLU]]
- [[Leaky ReLU]]
- [[SiLU]]
- [[Tanh]]
