[[Vanishing Gradients|Vanishing gradients]] occur when gradients become exponentially small during backpropagation in deep networks, hindering training.

Common in saturating activations like [[Sigmoid]] or [[Tanh]].

Mitigated by [[ReLU]], residuals, normalization, etc.
